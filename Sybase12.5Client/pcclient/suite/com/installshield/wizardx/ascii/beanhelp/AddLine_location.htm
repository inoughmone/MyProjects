Location determines where in the ASCII file to add text or a line(s).

Values

  • "Start of File" - The text or line(s) will be added at the beginning of the ASCII file.
  • "End of File" - The text or line(s) will be added to the end of the ASCII file.
  • "Current Search Result" - The text or line(s) will be added at the location(s) specified by the last Search Line/Text action.
